## 📚 Educational Resources

### **Why Invest in Precious Metals?**
- **Inflation hedge**: Metals hold value when currency weakens
- **Portfolio diversification**: Low correlation to stocks
- **Safe haven**: Stable during market downturns
- **Industrial demand**: Silver has manufacturing uses

### **Gold vs Silver Performance**
- **Gold**: More stable, smaller price swings, "fear" investment
- **Silver**: More volatile, 70% industrial use, "greed" investment
- **Correlation**: Both move together but silver more extreme

### **Long-term Outlook**
- **GLD/SLV**: Track commodity prices, passive investment
- **GOLD/SILVER**: Can outperform on mining efficiency and dividends
- **Best holding period**: 5+ years for precious metals
